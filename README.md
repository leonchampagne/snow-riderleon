# 🎿 Snow Rider - Gift Edition

A fun and addictive skiing game where you collect gifts and avoid obstacles!

## How to Play

### Controls
- **← → Arrow Keys** or **A/D** - Move left and right
- Click **START GAME** to begin

### Gameplay
- 🎁 **Collect Gifts** - Grab pink gift boxes for 50 points
- 🌲 **Avoid Obstacles** - Brown obstacles end your game
- ⬆️ **Stay Safe** - Pass obstacles to gain 10 points
- ⬆️ **Level Up** - Collect 5 gifts to advance to the next level (increases difficulty)

## Features
✨ Smooth animations and controls
❄️ Falling snowflakes background
🎨 Colorful graphics and design
📊 Score, gift, and level tracking
🎮 Progressive difficulty system
🚀 Fast-paced gameplay
